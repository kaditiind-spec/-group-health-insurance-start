1. Defining Your GHI Eligibility Matrix

Easily control VIS budget limits and maximize insurance utilization.

Who is Eligible

  • ✔ Full-time confirmed employees: Core roster driving VIS growth.
  • ✔ Minimum 7 active lives: Requirement by IRDAI to register. Can include immediate dependents.
  • ✔ Existing core team: Activated immediately to celebrate the corporate milestone!

Initially Excluded

  • ✘ Interns & Short-term hires: Exclude to avoid paying unrecoverable setup fees.
  • ✘ Freelancers & Consultants: Recommended to manage on normal invoice fee-terms.
  • ✘ Part-time / Temp roles: Keeps VIS administration lean and completely compliant.

Why this is best practice: This balanced setup prevents high premium outlays on staff who might transition quickly. It establishes a standard, high-value corporate badge for your key builders.

Why a 90-Day Probation Window?

Enrolling employees on Day 1 can inflate insurance costs for startups because of administrative overhead and early-stage dropouts.

1
Saves company cost from temporary trial hires.
2
Adds strong psychological incentive to complete probation.
3
Perfectly standard practice for fast-growing Indian organizations.

CRITICAL HR REQUIREMENT: The CD Account

To guarantee instant additions, VIS will maintain a **CD (Continuous Deposit) Account** with the insurer (often managed seamlessly inside modern dashboards like Plum). As soon as an employee completes their 90 days, the premium is automatically deducted from this pre-funded balance, issuing their active card within hours.

Step A

Keep ₹15,000 as a revolving safety deposit in the CD balance.

Step B

HR updates employee list on the portal weekly.

Step C

Policy active. Direct cashless health cards issued instantly.

Aditi's Guide: Questions to Ask Insurance Providers

Ask these exact questions when you connect with the shortlisted brokers to secure the best deal:

Pre-Existing Diseases

"Is there a waiting period for Pre-Existing Diseases (PED), or are they covered from Day 1 of employee activation?"

CD Account Minimums

"What is the minimum reserve balance required in our CD Account to trigger post-probation additions instantly?"

Cashless Hospital Cap

"What is your cashless settlement turnaround time (TAT), and what network hospitals do you support near our main hubs?"
